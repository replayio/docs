## What is Replay.io?

![Replay DevTools](/images/devtools_6hkjfs.png)

Replay.io is a time-travelling DevTool and debugger. It has all the similarities with your favourite browser DevTools. But instead of running your application live, you create a recording of your application and do debugging of the recording.

We like to call this recording *a replay*, since it is not simply a video or a DOM trace, but a complete runtime capture of your application. Because of this, you get much more insight into your code than with traditional DevTools. For example you can see:

## Common use cases
- :SquareIcon{name="i-heroicons-cursor-arrow-rays-20-solid" } **[Local debugging:]{class="text-primary"}** developers use Replay to better understand how their application works and debug difficult bugs.
- :SquareIcon{name="i-heroicons-bug-ant-16-solid"} **[Bug reports:]{class="text-primary"}** teams use Replay to record bug reports in their Support and QA workflows so that developers can debug the issues without having to reproduce it locally.
- :SquareIcon{name="i-ic-baseline-checklist"} **[Flaky and failing tests:]{class="text-primary"}** teams use Replay to record their end-to-end tests in CI so that developers can debug flaky and failed tests.
